# Workflow Trigger Issues

**Status:** Archived  
**Project:** Lead and purchase automation flow  
**Tags:** #chat-archive #workflow #triggers #automation #ghl #foundation-course

## Objective
Identify and fix trigger issues affecting lead routing, purchase tagging, or access automation.

## Main Issue
Parts of the workflow were not always triggering as expected, creating uncertainty around whether leads, tags, or paid-access steps were firing correctly.

## Key Discussion Points
- User needed confidence that the automation path worked end to end
- Trigger issues may have affected lead flow, purchase flow, or follow-up delivery
- The system included multiple connected steps, making one broken trigger hard to spot quickly

## Likely Workflow Areas
- lead capture form submission
- tag application
- purchase confirmation
- paid access email
- portal unlock or login flow

## Main Insight
Trigger issues needed to be documented clearly because a small break in automation can affect conversions, access, and reporting.

## Next Step
Create a trigger map listing each event, what should fire next, and how to verify it manually.
