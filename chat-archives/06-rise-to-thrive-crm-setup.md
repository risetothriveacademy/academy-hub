# Rise to Thrive CRM Setup

**Status:** Archived  
**Project:** Lead routing / GHL / video and playlist support  
**Tags:** #chat-archive #crm #ghl #youtube #leads #foundation-course

## Objective
Ensure leads from content and ads route correctly into the CRM and then into the Foundation Course sales and access flow.

## Main Discussion Points
- User asked whether the video page should work from start to finish
- There was discussion around whether all ads could use the same video page and lead form path
- User was clarifying how symptom-specific ads connect into a shared video explanation page and then into the Foundation Course funnel

## Funnel Concept Discussed
Ad by symptom  
→ video explanation page  
→ GHL lead capture  
→ Foundation Course sales page  
→ enrollment / purchase  
→ automation / access

## Key Questions Raised
- Is it acceptable to use one common video page for all ad traffic?
- Do all YouTube leads go through one specific email / form setup?
- Was the first video created before the form setup was finalized?

## Main Conclusion
A shared bridge page can work, as long as:
- tracking is clear
- lead capture works
- downstream automation is correct
- ad source attribution remains understandable

## Next Step
Document the exact CRM trigger and tagging path for YouTube leads versus other paid or organic channels.
